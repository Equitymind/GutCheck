# GutCheck

GutCheck is a real-time authenticity signal platform that analyzes how a viewer responds to video content and compares that response to the behavior of the video itself.

## What it does

- Captures real-time viewer response during video playback  
- Extracts behavioral signals from the video content  
- Compares the two using temporal correlation  
- Surfaces an authenticity signal based on alignment and divergence  

## Why it matters

Most detection systems analyze the video.

GutCheck analyzes the relationship between the video and the viewer.

This enables detection of inconsistencies that are not visible at the pixel or artifact level.
