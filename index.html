Why It Works

Your body detects what your eyes miss

Humans have evolved over thousands of years to detect subtle inconsistencies in behavior, often before we can explain why.

GutCheck taps into that system.

By measuring your real-time reactions and comparing them to the video itself, it surfaces signals traditional analysis misses.

And when multiple people react the same way at the same moment, that pattern becomes impossible to ignore.
